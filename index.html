CORONATION 2016

Bengaluru has the privilege of hosting CORONATION 2016, the Annual Conference of District 92, Toastmasters International. District 92 consists of around 150+ Toastmasters Clubs located in the States of Karnataka and Kerala, India. The conference will be held from 20th to 22nd May, 2016, at the Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield, a premier five-star destination located in the heart of Bengaluru’s IT center.

This Mega Event will bring together over 500 Toastmasters from across Karnataka and Kerala to learn, teach, and share communication and leadership skills, and experiences. The participants include CEOs, Entrepreneurs, HR Professionals, Trainers, and Students from among the best companies, organizations, and education institutions in India. The objective of CORONATION 2016 is to promote Educational Programs, Speech Contests, and Networking.

Among them will be nine of India’s finest Toastmasters who will compete to be the next District Champion representing District 92 at the World Championship of Public Speaking at the 2016 Toastmasters International Convention at Washington DC, USA. CORONATION 2016 will have educational sessions conducted by renowned national and international trainers, networking opportunities, and fun activities spread over three action packed days. Be sure not to miss it!
